<idea>
  **Design Movement**: "Neo-Brutalist Command Center"
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Raw Functionality**: Exposed grids, monospaced typography, and high-contrast borders.
  2. **Data Density**: Maximizing information per pixel without clutter, using strict tabular layouts.
  3. **Systemic Transparency**: Visualizing the "machine" at work—scan status, API latency, and raw data feeds.
  **Color Philosophy**: Stark black and white with a single, electric accent color (e.g., Hyper-Green or Signal Orange). Represents precision, urgency, and the "terminal" aesthetic of high-frequency trading.
  **Layout Paradigm**: Modular bento-box grid. Fixed sidebars and headers with independently scrolling data panels. No soft shadows; hard lines only.
  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Marquee Tickers**: Scrolling text for live market updates.
  - **Brutal Borders**: Thick, 2px-3px borders defining every component.
  - **Glitch Accents**: Subtle CSS glitch effects on hover states or alerts.
  **Interaction Philosophy**: Instantaneous, tactile feedback. Buttons depress visibly (transform: translate). Hover states invert colors completely.
  **Animation**: Snappy, linear transitions (0.1s). No easing curves. Elements "snap" into place.
  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: 'Space Mono' or 'JetBrains Mono' (Uppercase, tracking-wide).
  - **Body**: 'Inter' or 'Roboto' (for readability in dense tables), but kept strictly utilitarian.
</idea>

  **Design Movement**: "Glassmorphic Fintech"
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Optical Depth**: Layered interfaces using background blur (backdrop-filter) to establish hierarchy.
  2. **Fluidity**: Soft gradients and rounded corners to make complex data feel approachable and "liquid."
  3. **Luminous Data**: Charts and graphs that glow against dark backgrounds, emphasizing the "gold" in the data.
  **Color Philosophy**: Deep midnight blues and charcoals as the base, with gradients of violet, cyan, and emerald for data visualization. Evokes the feeling of a premium, late-night trading desk.
  **Layout Paradigm**: Floating cards on a mesh-gradient background. Sidebar is a translucent dock.
  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Frosted Glass Cards**: White/Black with low opacity and blur.
  - **Glow Effects**: Drop shadows that match the element's color (colored shadows).
  - **Mesh Gradients**: Subtle, animated background blobs that shift slowly.
  **Interaction Philosophy**: Smooth, weightless interactions. Hover states lift elements (transform: translateY) and increase glow.
  **Animation**: Slow, fluid easing (0.4s). Elements float and drift.
  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: 'Plus Jakarta Sans' (Geometric, modern).
  - **Body**: 'DM Sans' (Clean, legible at small sizes).
</idea>

  **Design Movement**: "Swiss International Style (Digital)"
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Grid Precision**: Absolute adherence to a mathematical grid system.
  2. **Objective Clarity**: Photography and data are presented without decoration. Form follows function.
  3. **Typographic Hierarchy**: Size and weight of text are the primary tools for organization, not boxes or borders.
  **Color Philosophy**: White background, black text, and a limited palette of "Signal" colors (Red, Blue, Yellow) used strictly for functional status (Alert, Info, Success).
  **Layout Paradigm**: Asymmetric grid. Large negative space to guide the eye. Content is king.
  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Huge Typography**: Page titles are massive (6xl+).
  - **Rule Lines**: Thin, hairline dividers (1px) to separate sections.
  - **Iconography**: Minimalist, stroke-based icons (Lucide).
  **Interaction Philosophy**: Subtle and refined. Minimal motion. Focus is on the content changing, not the container.
  **Animation**: Fade-ins and slide-ups. Very subtle.
  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: 'Helvetica Now' or 'Inter' (Tight tracking, bold weights).
  - **Body**: 'Inter' (Regular weight, high contrast).
</idea>
